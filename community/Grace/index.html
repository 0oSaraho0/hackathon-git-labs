About Me

I'm an student software developer that is extremely passionate about making society much more accessible for people with disabilities and neurodiversity, and hope to be able to go into a job where I can use both that passionate and my skills as a developer to make a difference. In my ideal world, neurodiversity is seen as a strength, an added bonus to a candidate for a job and not something that would put off an employer or school. It's my dream to make education and work more accessible for those that are ND so that they don't have to struggle as I have over the years.
